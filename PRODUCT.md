# Product

## Register

product

## Users
School Admins (educators/teachers), Student Teams (performing design-thinking tasks in classrooms, often with shared low-resource devices), field team staff (Teacher Trainers, Geography Leads), and State Education Department (SED) observers.

## Product Purpose
A collaborative workspace to empower and democratize design-driven education and Computational Thinking across schools in India, allowing ideas to progress through structured Design Thinking validation stages while ensuring strict geographical scoping, session reports tracking, and lab-safety compliance audits.

## Brand Personality
Professional, inspiring, empathetic, and premium. Characterized by high-fidelity glassmorphism, responsive micro-animations, and dynamic HSL fluid orbs.

## Anti-references
- Side-stripe borders greater than 1px as a colored accent on cards, lists, or alerts.
- Gradient text styles combining background-clip with transparent text colors.
- Overuse of glassmorphism as a default (reserved for structural overlays, not generic card backgrounds).
- Standard SaaS-cream metric templates (Big number, small label, gradient accent).
- Repetitive identical card grids.
- Modals as a default first-thought interaction pattern.
- Double-hyphens or em-dashes for separators.

## Design Principles
1. **Contextual Simplicity**: Onboarding and authentication flows must accommodate low-resource, shared classroom devices (e.g., student PIN-based bypass logins).
2. **Visual Contrast & Hierarchy**: Ensure a high-contrast experience while maintaining rich, harmonious HSL teal/blue color tones.
3. **Structured Validation**: Progression through the Design Thinking pipeline must feel physical, utilizing step indicators and card-drag animations.
4. **Absolute Geographical Isolation**: Data isolation and rollups must be completely secure and invisible across school and district tiers.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Ensure WCAG compliance for high-contrast visibility, support screen readers, and provide smooth non-elastic layout transition effects.
